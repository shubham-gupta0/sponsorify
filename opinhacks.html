Hackathon

OpinHacks

Description

OpInHacks will be a 36-hour student-run hackathon which will not only cover tech tracks but also non-tech tracks, considering the more comprehensive section of enthusiastic coders. This hackathon welcomes all, regardless of their gender or background, and not only aims to develop a competitive drive in the participants but also a welcoming space for them to come and challenge themselves and grow as a developer.
